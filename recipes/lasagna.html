Delicious Lasagna!

Recipe Description

Ingredients

  • 1 pound sweet Italian sausage
  • ¾ pound lean ground beef
  • ½ cup minced onion
  • 2 cloves garlic, crushed

Steps:

  1. This classic lasagna is made with an easy meat sauce as the base.
  2. Layer the sauce with noodles and cheese, then bake until bubbly!
  3. This is great for feeding a big family, and freezes well, too.
